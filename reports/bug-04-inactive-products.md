# Bug Report: Inactive Products Disappear from List

## 📄 Description
When a product is marked as "Inactive", it is completely removed from the list and cannot be found even with filters, making reactivation impossible without a new registration.

## ✅ Expected Behavior
Inactive products should be hidden from *new orders* but remain visible/editable in the product management list for future reactivation.

## ❌ Actual Result
The product is deleted or permanently hidden from the UI after inactivation.

## 🔁 Steps to Reproduce
1. Log in as **Admin** > **Registration** > **Products**.
2. Select a product and set it to **Inactive**.
3. Try to locate the product in the list using the "Inactive" filter.

## 🔥 Severity
🟡 **Medium**
