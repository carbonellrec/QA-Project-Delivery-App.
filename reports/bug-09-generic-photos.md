# Bug Report: Non-Specific Photo Attachment in Visit Checklist

## 📄 Description
The visit checklist allows photo attachments but does not distinguish between "Regular Product Count" and "Losses," making it difficult to audit specific items.

## ✅ Expected Behavior
Photos should be linked specifically to either the "Product Count" list or the "Losses" list for accurate visual verification.

## ❌ Actual Result
The system provides a generic photo upload at the bottom of the checklist, mixing all visual evidence together.

## 🔁 Steps to Reproduce
1. Log in as **Promoter**.
2. Start a **Visit** > **Checklist**.
3. Add items to both "Product Count" and "Losses".
4. Attempt to attach a photo specifically to a "Loss" item.

## 🔥 Severity
🟢 **Low**
